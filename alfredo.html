Pasta Alfredo

An absolute classic, this recipe is both delicious and easy to make

Ingredients (Easy Way)

  • Pasta (Spaghetti or Fettucine variety preferred)
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Chicken (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add a pinch of salt and olive oil to the boil
  3. Stir in pasta (unbroken mind you) and set timer for 10-12 minutes
  4. Place alfredo sauce in another pot on medium heat
  5. With good timing, the timer will go off and the sauce/pasta will be done
  6. Add alfredo sauce to pasta, serve

You're all done!
